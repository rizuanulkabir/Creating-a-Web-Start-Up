> **"We give small companies big buying power."** > **"Buy together. Save together."**

GroupBuy is the first digital co-buying platform tailored specifically for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs). Our platform connects SMEs across various fragmented industries—such as gastronomy, construction, garments, and office supplies—enabling them to bundle their purchasing power and secure bulk-order discounts that are usually reserved for large corporations.

---

## ⚠️ The Problem

* **For Small Businesses (Buyers):** Fragmented SMEs miss out on massive supplier discounts because they cannot meet high Minimum Order Quantities (MOQs). Over a business lifetime, this can lead to accumulated losses of up to **€648,000** per business.
* **For Suppliers:** Reaching and managing thousands of individual small businesses is highly inefficient. Furthermore, suppliers lack reliable aggregated data for advanced production and demand planning.

## 💡 Our Solution

* **Buyer Side:** GroupBuy automatically connects local SMEs to bundle orders, unlocking steep quantity discounts (typically saving **16% to 30%**) without the coordination headaches.
* **Supplier Side:** We provide a streamlined digital sales channel that aggregates high-volume orders. Our platform incorporates AI-driven historical pattern analytics to help suppliers predict market trends and seamlessly plan production.

---

## 🚀 Key Features & Value Proposition

### 🏢 For SMEs
* **Bulk Purchasing Power:** Instantly scale your buying clout by standing together with other businesses.
* **Real Price Reduction:** Transparent tiered pricing structures that lower costs as more buyers join a deal.
* **Simplified Logistics:** Automated coordination across centralized drop-off points or integrated 3PL networks.

### 🏭 For Suppliers
* **Expanded Network Access:** Easily tap into an aggregated ecosystem of local SMEs without extra marketing spend.
* **Demand Planning Security:** AI-driven analytics that forecast customer ordering frequencies and volume requirements.
* **Predictable Revenue:** Bulk upfront commitments minimize the financial volatility of fragmented orders.

## 💼 Business Model

GroupBuy uses a sustainable, performance-driven monetization strategy:
1. **Success Fee (Buyer Side):** We take a **10% fee out of the actual savings** generated for the buyer. If you don't save money, you don't pay.
2. **Supplier Freemium Model:** Core listing features are free, with premium subscriptions unlocking predictive data analytics and priority placement.
3. **Premium Monetization:** Advertising tools and featured slots for suppliers looking to push inventory quickly.
